Overview

Purpose

To make a more enjoyable experience for potential customers and create a platform on which they can live out all of their white water rafting hopes and dreams.

Audience

My audience is young-ish, excitable-ish, sad-ish people who are looking for something to give them purpose in life. Let the rapids take your spirit on an adventure. Is this you?

Do you feel an underlying sense of purposelessness in your life? Do you try to escape the dullness of everyday life by scrolling for hours on TikTok, Instagram, or Facebook? Do you wish you were happier? Come on down to the rapids. We'll slosh you into joy!
